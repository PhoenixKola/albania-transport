# Privacy Policy — Tirana Transport

_Last updated: 2026-03-01_

Tirana Transport (“the App”) helps you browse Tirana public transport routes and stops, view scheduled departures, save favorites, and see nearby stops.

## What data we collect
**We do not ask for or collect personal information** such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.

## Data stored on your device
The App stores the following **locally on your device** (on-device storage) to provide core functionality and remember your preferences:
- Selected language (Albanian/English)
- Favorite routes and stops
- Cached schedule/feed files (downloaded GTFS-derived JSON) for offline use

This data stays on your device and is not sent to us.

## Location
The App includes an optional “Nearby Stops” feature.
- If you grant permission, the App uses your **approximate device location** to calculate which stops are closest to you.
- Your location is processed **on-device** and is **not** sent to us.

You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.

## Data the App fetches
The App fetches public transport schedule data from publicly available sources:
- Static GTFS feed: `https://pt.tirana.al/gtfs/gtfs.zip`
- The App may also fetch a publicly hosted, generated JSON feed derived from the GTFS data (for faster downloads).

The App uses these sources to display routes, stops, and scheduled departures.

## Advertising
If you enable ads in the App, it may display ads and use third-party advertising SDKs (for example, Google Mobile Ads / AdMob).

These SDKs may automatically collect certain device and usage information in order to serve and measure ads. This may include:
- IP address (used for network communication)
- Approximate location inferred from IP (depending on the ad network’s settings)
- Ad performance and interaction data (e.g., impressions, clicks)
- Basic device information (such as device model, OS version)

We do not use this data to identify you personally.

You can reset or limit ad personalization from your device settings:
- Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads (wording may vary by Android version)

## Analytics
The App does not use a separate in-app analytics product to track you (beyond what advertising SDKs may collect for ad measurement).

## Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. However, ad providers (if used) may process certain data (described above) to provide advertising and measure performance.

## Security
The App does not use accounts or passwords. It downloads only public transport schedule data and stores only local preferences and cached data on your device.

## Children’s privacy
The App is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
